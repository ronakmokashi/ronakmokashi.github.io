Application Lifecycle Management

  • by Ronak Mokashi

SwiftALM is a most comprehensive product for managing IT and Software projects in the Global Delivery Model. SwiftALM comes with a unique combination of features to manage traditional and Agile projects for a wide variety of industries. It combines Process Governance, Program/ Project Management and Software Lifecycle Management as well as a comprehensive set of Integrations to deliver this capability. - credits, digite.com

With this we brought the SwiftALM product experience to the mobile. Now people could track their work-items on the go. Also perform different kinds of actions over it. Assign work to your team member, monitor them, etc.

We also made the dashboard widgets possible on the mobile where you could monitor your progress and what your team is upto just by swiping down the widgets. Time tracking was also an essential feature for the mobile. Now users could easily updated and view their time sheet entries on the go hassle free.

Swift ALM mobile app is not native yet, so we had to make sure that our designs on the web will be responsive enough on mobile. Now this is surely a common thing today and there’s nothing so great about it but that goes for a product app or website. What we were working on was a huge enterprise level application and then streamlining those important features into mobile was a task. We carefully had to nudge few details to fit everything in the smaller screens. We never wanted the user to feel that gap or difference while using both the mediums and we were successful in doing the same.
